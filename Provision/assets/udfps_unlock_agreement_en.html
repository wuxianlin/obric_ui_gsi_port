Welcome to use the fingerprint unlocking screen feature.

About this feature:

Audio fingerprint recognition is a technology that identifies a person's identity based on their fingerprint characteristic information. This technology captures the user's fingerprint data through the built-in fingerprint sensor in the mobile phone (such as under-screen optical fingerprint, under-screen ultrasonic fingerprint, etc.) and compares it with the pre-registered fingerprint characteristics, thereby achieving audio fingerprint recognition and identity verification. Audio fingerprint recognition can be used to unlock the device screen.

Precautions:

Fingerprint can be used to unlock devices easily, but its security may be less reliable than that of complex passwords, pattern passwords, or numeric passwords.

Since you leave fingerprints when touching objects, others may use your fingerprints to unlock this device after replicating them. Do not casually hand over the device to others to prevent the audio fingerprint recognition function from being maliciously exploited.

Data Privacy Statement for Audio Fingerprint Recognition Unlock Function:

Your fingerprint data, including the mathematical representation of your fingerprint, will be strictly protected and encrypted for storage in a secure isolated area of this device. We will not back up your fingerprint data, and other applications will not be able to access or back up your fingerprint data. Fingerprint data is not stored in image form but exists in the form of a mathematical representation, and no one can obtain your original fingerprint image. In addition, fingerprint data will be continuously updated as you use the fingerprint unlocking function to improve your experience and enhance security.

You have the authority to manage independently and can control the scope of use of audio fingerprint recognition by yourself. For example, you can go to ["System Settings" > "Password & Security" > "Password Settings" > "Lock Screen Password"] to set the lock screen password method and turn off the function of unlocking the screen with audio fingerprint recognition.

You can also manage your fingerprint information (including enrolling new fingerprints and deleting the fingerprint data you have already enrolled) via <"System Settings" > "Password & Security" > "Fingerprint Information Management">. Once deleted, the relevant data will be immediately cleared from the device. If you have not enrolled any fingerprints or refuse to provide fingerprint information, the fingerprint unlock function will be unavailable.
