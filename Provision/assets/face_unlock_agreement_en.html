Welcome to use the facial unlock screen feature.

About this feature:

Facial recognition is a technology that identifies a person's identity based on facial feature information. This technology captures multiple frames of images in real time through the front-facing camera of a mobile phone, ultimately forming a set of facial feature data as reference data. When you perform facial unlocking, this function will compare real-time facial feature data, thereby quickly completing the comparison and identity authentication, which can be used to unlock the screen. Accordingly, we need to apply for and call the front-facing camera permission of the device to capture facial data.

Precautions for enabling facial unlock function:

Your device may be unlocked by someone with a similar appearance to you. Do not casually hand your device over to others to prevent this feature from being maliciously exploited;

The security of facial unlock is lower than that of pattern passwords, numeric passwords, and complex passwords;

When entering facial data, avoid the following personal states, such as wearing sunglasses, hats, masks, having a beard, or wearing heavy makeup;

Avoid entering information in dimly lit environments, prevent the camera lens from getting dirty, and ensure the headshot is clear and easily recognizable to increase the probability of successful matching.

Data Privacy Statement for Facial Unlock Function:

Your facial data will be strictly protected and encrypted, stored in a secure isolated area of this device. We will not back up your facial data, and other applications will not be able to access or back up your facial data either.
