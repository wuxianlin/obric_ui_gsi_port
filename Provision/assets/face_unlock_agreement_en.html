About this feature:

Face recognition is a technology that identifies individuals based on facial characteristics. It uses the device's front camera to capture multiple frames in real time and generates a set of facial feature data as baseline data. When you use Face Unlock, the feature compares real-time facial feature data to quickly complete matching and authentication, allowing you to unlock the screen. Accordingly, we need to request and use the device's front camera permission to capture facial data.

Important notes for enabling Face Unlock:

Your device may be unlocked by someone whose appearance is similar to yours. Do not hand your device to others casually to prevent misuse of this feature.

Face Unlock is less secure than pattern, numeric, or complex passwords.

When enrolling your face, avoid the following conditions: wearing sunglasses, hats, masks, having a beard, or heavy makeup.

Avoid enrolling in dim lighting and ensure the camera lens is clean. Make sure your face is clear and easily recognizable to improve the matching success rate.

Data privacy statement for Face Unlock:

Your facial data, including its mathematical representation, is strictly protected and stored in encrypted form within the device's secure isolated area. We do not back up your facial data, and other applications cannot access or back up your facial data. This data becomes more accurate and is continuously updated as you use Face Unlock to improve your experience and enhance security. When a highly matched face is detected but the device is subsequently unlocked with a password, the facial data will also be updated.

You have full control to manage this feature. You can disable the Face Unlock screen in [System Settings > Password & Security > Enroll Face Information > Enter home screen after unlock] or [System Settings > Password & Security > Password Settings (Lock screen password)].

You can also delete your enrolled facial data via [System Settings > Password & Security > Enroll Face Information]. Once deleted, the device will no longer retain your facial information, and related features will become unavailable.
